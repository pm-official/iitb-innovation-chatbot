## ENT605: Business Fundamentals for Technopreneurs
- Credits: 3
- Type: Theory
- Description: Businesses today are very complex and multi-disciplinary operations. Aspiring entrepreneurs and managers need to have a broad based overview of how companies operate and succeed. This course introduces the Fundamentals of Business to the learners.  1. Business and Business Environment 2. Company Structure, Goals, Strategy & Values 3. New Product Development, Manufacturing & Quality Management 4. Leading Teams & Human Resource Management 5. Marketing. Branding & Advertising 6. B2B / B2C Sales & Distribution Channels 7. Customer Service & Advocacy. 8. Supply Chain & Logistics 9. Finance & Accounting 10. Environment Society & Governance

## ENT606: Developing a Proof of Concept- Basic
- Credits: 6
- Type: Laboratory
- Description: ENT 606 Developing a Proof of Concept (Basic) is a foundational lab course for aspiring entrepreneurs - it is a project-based course and involves workshops in the lab where students gain hands-on experience in fundamental skills required for product development. The expected output is a product designed after learning from the workshops, and having undergone concept and product development for your start-up ideas. Both the concept and the product will be considered and evaluated for the course grade. Learning Objectives : (1) Hands-on approach to model building (2) Prototype-Design Approaches (3) Creation of working prototype (4) Testing of prototype
