# Earth Sciences - Course Catalog for IIT Bombay Students


## GP 101: Solid-Earth Geophysics
- Credits: 6
- Type: Theory
- Prerequisite: NIL
- Description: The Solar System and others terrestrial planets. Propagation of Elastic waves inside the Earth, Composition and Internal structure of the Earth. Spherical harmonics. Role of satellites for Geopotential fields. Gravity: shape, rotation, tidal deformation, isostasy of the Earth. Heat flow: continental and ocean heat flow, thermal structure of the Earth. Radiometric dating methods. Convection in the Mantle and Outer Core. Geomagnetism: origin of the Earth-s magnetic field, geomagnetic secular variation, Geodynamo. Plate Tectonics: Paleomagnetism, Continental Drift, Sea-floor spreading and polar wander paths.

## GP 401: Global Geophysics
- Credits: 4
- Type: Theory
- Description: The Solar system and evolution of the Earth; Structure and composition of the earth   Geodynamics. Theory and fundamental concepts of Plate Tectonics: Rotation vectors, rotation poles, present day plate motions and triple junctions. Geophysical framework of the earth: Spherical harmonic representation of potential functions. Gravity: figure of the earth, shape, rotation, tidal friction, gravity field of earth and Isostasy. Geomagnetism: internal, external field & their causes, geodynamo theory. Paleomagnetism: Polar wander paths, Seafloor spreading and Continental drift. Heat flow: heat transfer mechanism, thermal regime, thermal structure of the earth, volcanism, Hot spots and mantle plumes. Elastic waves: internal structure and variation physical properties in the interior of the earth.
