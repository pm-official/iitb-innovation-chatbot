## DH 604: R&D Project-I
- Credits: 6
- Type: Project
- Description: The DH 604 - Research and Development Project course offers students a unique opportunity to undertake hands-on research and development in the field of Digital Health under the mentorship of faculty members associated with KCDH, IIT Bombay. Projects will focus on 6-7 research areas. Students are expected to contribute meaningfully to a research project and/or development work, gaining practical exposure to various methods, tools, and problem-solving strategies. Evaluation is based on contribution to the project, project reports, presentations, and final deliverables as decided by the respective faculty mentor.
