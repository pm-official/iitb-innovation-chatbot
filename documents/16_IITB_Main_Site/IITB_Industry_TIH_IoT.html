Technology Innovation Hub (TIH) at IIT Bombay is a Section 8 Company set up through the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS) to boost new and emerging technologies in the areas of internet of things (IoT) and IoE. It is bringing out several technological solutions for people-centric problems.
