Smart India Hackathon

  • Start Up Contest
  • Clean & Smart Campus Award 
  • Aicte -CII India Innovation Initiative(I3)
  • Smart India Hackathon
  • Aicte National Level Programs 
  • UKEIRI 2020

Smart India Hackathon is a nationwide initiative to provide students a platform to solve some of pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem solving. 

 

The last edition of the hackathon saw over 5 million+ students from various engineering colleges compete for the top prize at 35+ locations.

 

In SIH, the students would also have the opportunity to work on challenges faced within the private sector organizations and create world class solutions for some of the top companies in the world, thus helping the Private sector hire the best minds from across the nation. 

 

Click Here for Invitation to Private & Public Sector industries and NGOs to join Smart India Hackathon (SIH)

 

For more details please visit https://sih.gov.in/
