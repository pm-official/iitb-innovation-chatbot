IIT Bombay Faculty Overview for Innovation and Entrepreneurship

IIT Bombay has 869+ faculty members across multiple departments.
Students working on startup ideas can reach out to professors whose
research areas align with their innovation domain.

HOW TO APPROACH A PROFESSOR:
1. Email them introducing yourself and your idea (keep it concise)
2. Mention how their research area connects to your startup idea
3. Ask if they would be willing to be a faculty mentor/advisor
4. DSSE courses are a great way to connect with entrepreneurship faculty
5. SINE incubation applications benefit from having a faculty advisor
