# Aerospace Visiting Department - Faculty Profiles for Innovation & Entrepreneurship


## Dr. A. Sivathanu Pillai
- Department: Aerospace Visiting
- Designation: Honorary Distinguished Professor (DRDO Retired)
- Email: aspillai@aero.iitb.ac.in
- Research Group: Strategic Technology and Missile Systems
- Primary Research Area: Strategic Management — Missile Technology & Defense Program Management
- Research Interests & Innovation Focus: Research on strategic technology development, guided missile systems, launch vehicle technology, management of large-scale high-technology defense programs. Key figure in BrahMos supersonic cruise missile development and India's missile program.
- Notable Projects: BrahMos supersonic cruise missile development; India's missile program; Author of books on technology management; Building High Technology Capabilities in India (IETE Technical Review)
- Key Tools & Platforms: Systems engineering, program management, technology strategy, strategic planning
- Industry Collaborations: BrahMos Aerospace (Chief Executive & Managing Director); ISRO; DRDO; Indian defense sector
- Awards: Padma Bhushan (2013); Padma Shri (2002); Distinguished Scientist DRDO; Known as 'Father of BrahMos'; Close associate of former President APJ Abdul Kalam
- Innovation Keywords: missile technology, strategic management, BrahMos, DRDO, launch vehicles, technology strategy, defense systems
- Profile: https://www.aero.iitb.ac.in/home/people/faculty

## Prof. Bhupendra Khandelwal
- Department: Aerospace Visiting
- Designation: Visiting Faculty (Assoc. Prof., University of Alabama)
- Email: bhupendra.khandelwal@aero.iitb.ac.in
- Research Group: Low Carbon Combustion Centre (LCCC)
- Primary Research Area: Propulsion — Alternative Aviation Fuels, Low-Emissions Combustion & Sustainability
- Research Interests & Innovation Focus: Research on performance evaluation of alternative aviation fuels, emissions measurement and reduction, low-emissions combustor development, fuel characterization, and combustor design optimization for sustainable aviation.
- Notable Projects: National Jet Fuel Combustion Program (NJFCP); CLEEN program; FlexJET; JETSCREEN; Best Paper Aeronautical Journal 2020
- Key Tools & Platforms: Combustor test facilities, emissions measurement, fuel characterization, combustion diagnostics, computational combustion
- Industry Collaborations: Rolls-Royce; Siemens; UK Dstl; FAA; Alternative fuel development
- Awards: Royal Aeronautical Society Centennial Scholar Award (2012); AIAA Best Paper (2019); ASME IGTI Student Scholar; Chartered Engineer (CEng); AIAA Senior Member
- Innovation Keywords: alternative aviation fuels, low-emissions combustion, sustainable aviation fuel, emissions control, fuel characterization, combustor design, bio-fuels
- Profile: https://www.aero.iitb.ac.in/home/people/faculty/bhupendra
