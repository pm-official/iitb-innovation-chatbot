Applied Science Park for Innovation Research & Entrepreneurship​
ASPIRE, the place where the seeds of innovation are sown, nurtured, and cultivated to fruition.
Established with the mission to connect research with real-world applications, ASPIRE has evolved into a vibrant platform that accelerates innovation, fosters research-driven collaborations, and transforms ideas into tangible impact.
The non-profit entity offers a space where academic brilliance converges with industry expertise. This is the story of ASPIRE, a journey that began with a vision to bridge the worlds of academia and industry, has grown into a thriving ecosystem where ideas are transformed into powerful outcomes.
Formation of Aspire​
In 2016, the Ministry of Education entrusted IIT Bombay with a pivotal mission: to establish the Research Park within the sprawling campus in Powai, Mumbai. This led to the creation of ASPIRE – the IIT Bombay Research Park Foundation, a non-profit organisation dedicated to catalysing innovation and research with real-world impact. Inspired by globally successful research park models, ASPIRE emerges as the epicenter, that connects knowledge, expertise, and resources seamlessly, fostering a strong and collaborative industry-academia ecosystem with a global outlook.
Our Vision
To be a leading global hub for technology innovation.
Our Mission
Fostering academia-industry collaboration for research translation.
Nurturing world-class talent in emerging technology areas.
Supporting the development of a vibrant deep tech entrepreneurship ecosystem
Our Values
Our ethos is built on integrity, excellence, accountability, transparency, and empathy. Intending to foster academia-industry collaboration, we strive for innovation while remaining accountable to stakeholders, operating transparently, and addressing societal needs with compassion.
What We Do
Cultivating R&D partnerships which evolve into a thriving innovation hub, advancing technologies with meaningful societal impact. Our industry-centric workspaces facilitate collaborative development, while students gain invaluable corporate research exposure, enriching the nation’s talent pool. It’s a dynamic ecosystem where academia, industry, and students converge to drive innovation and uphold academic excellence.
Indian Institute of Technology Bombay​​​
ASPIRE proudly calls IIT Bombay its home, a place recognized globally for its leadership in engineering education and research. The campus pulsates with the energy of exceptional faculty and bright young minds, fostering a culture of relentless innovation. ASPIRE invites industries to become an integral part of this vibrant ecosystem, tapping into the intellectual capital that defines IIT Bombay.
Driving Innovation Forward
Our team at ASPIRE comprises dedicated individuals driven by a shared passion for innovation and impact. With diverse expertise spanning various domains, we collaborate tirelessly to push the boundaries of possibility and shape a future defined by progress.

Industry - Academia Collaboration

2024
Inauguration of ASPIRE IIT Bombay Research Park Foundation
Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated the G+14 floor ASPIRE- IIT Bombay Research Park on February 20, 2024. The 5 lakh sq ft facility, with dignitaries present, signifies a pivotal step in promoting innovation and marks a significant milestone in advancing innovation and collaboration between academia and industry.
Indo - French R&D Seminar
The ‘Indo-French R&D Seminar,’ conducted by IIT Bombay Research Park Foundation-ASPIRE, in collaboration with the Indo-French Chamber of Commerce & Industry (IFCCI) and The French Institute in India, was a resounding success.

2022
Conclave on Sustainability
We hosted this with 194 attendees, featuring talks, parallel sessions, and a poster competition. This event was honoured by Chief Guest, Dr M. Ravichandran, Hon’ble Secretary, Ministry of Earth Sciences, Government of India. The panel discussion highlighted the need for sector-specific strategies and local approaches to address Sustainable Development Goals.
