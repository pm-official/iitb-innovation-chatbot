Centres of excellence - Indian Institute of Technology Bombay
Centres of Excellence / Research
IIT Bombay is an ‘Institute of Eminence’ which is at the forefront of scientific discovery and emerging technologies including Healthcare Informatics, Biosciences, Green Energy, Climate Change, Electric Mobility, Drone Technologies, applications of Artificial Intelligence and Data Sciences, and Digital Trust – all of which will have a profound impact. The Institute has opened several Centres of Excellence that will allow IIT Bombay to take the lead in emerging technologies in the country and help in nation-building.
